Short Biography

Presently, I am working as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics, School of Advanced Sciences, Vellore Institute of Technology, Vellore, Tamil Nadu, India with five years of teaching experience. My major research area is Fractal Analysis and other recent areas include

  • Fractal Interpolation Functions
  • Fractional Calculus
  • Nonliner Dynamics
  • Climate Change
  • Image Processing
  • Artificial Intelligence

I published more than 40 research articles in the reputed international journals inlcuding Fractals, Advances in Space Research, Results in Mathematics and The European Physical Journal Plus. I published three books namely Fractal Patterns in Nonlinear Dynamics and Applications (in CRC Press, Taylor & Francis Group, 2019), Fractal Functions, Dimensions and Signal Analysis (in Springer: Complexity, 2021) and Fractal Patterns with MATLAB (SpingerBriefs in Complexity, 2024). I was one of the editors for two edited books in CRC Press and Taylor & Francis Group. I was the guest editor for three special issues in The European Physical Journal Special Topics and Springer. I have been reviewing for more than 10 international journals and awarded "Distinguished EPJ Referees -2021".
